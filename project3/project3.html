MyNextLvl Project Topic

The goal of this project is to use a few different persona's and possible scenarios of potential real world users to speculate on how they would behave on the MyNextLvl website to identify any design flaws for possible negative user experience to create a better website.

Stakeholders:
- Providers
- Parents
- Users of the application (12-21 with chronic conditions)
- Product Owner
- Health Department in DeKalb County

User Groups:
- 12-17 Year Old Users (Madeline)
- 18-21 Year Old Users (Johnny)
- Parents (Deanna)

Persona's Created


Madeline Miller Age: 13

Bio: Madeline is a 13-year-old female who has been diagnosed with asthma. Since Maddy is under 18 years old she will need her parents’ verification to create an account on MyNextLvl. She and her parents may create an account together. She gets irritated when she forgets she must miss school to go see the doctor, so her parents want to give her a way to get reminders. She also wants to learn more about asthma and why she needs an inhaler and learn about her body’s limitations when she plays outside.
Madeline's Goals:
- Get help setting up an account from a parent.
- Learn more about her asthma condition and physical limitations.
- Use the website to set reminders for doctor appointments.


Johnny Lawrence
Age: 19
From Bartow, GA

Bio: Johnny is a 19-year-old young man who just graduated high school. He had been recently diagnosed with Hyperthyroidism. He wants a way to become more independent with his medical condition. He is looking for a way to organize his insurance information, doctor's appointments, and medications all together in one place.
Johnny's Goals:
- Johnny wants to create an account with MyNextLvl.
- Johnny wants to save his insurance information on the website.
- Johnny wants to save a list of his medications.
- Johnny wants to set reminders for appointments.


Deanna Colbert
Age: 36
From Summertown, GA

Bio: Deanna has a 5-year-old son who has been diagnosed with diabetes. She wants to become more familiar with the condition so she can help manage his condition the best way possible.

User Task List

Madeline's task
- Create an account with a parent
- Read the information provided on the website about diabetes.
- Set up reminders for doctor appointments.

Johnny's Task

- Upload photo of insurnace card to profile.
- Save medication list in profile.
