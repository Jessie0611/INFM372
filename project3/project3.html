Site Structure

The goal of this project is to use a few different persona's and possible scenarios of potential real-world users to speculate on how they would behave on the MyNextLvl website to identify any design flaws for possible negative user experience to create a better website.

Stakeholders:
- Providers
- Parents
- Users of the application (12-21 with chronic conditions)
- Product Owner
- Health Department in DeKalb County

User Groups:
- 12-17 Year Old Users (Madeline)
- 18-21 Year Old Users (Johnny)
- Parents (Deanna)


User Task List

- Users want to create an account.
- Users want to learn about their medical condition.
- Users want to learn about local resources available.
- Users want to add/edit/remove an appointment to their doctor appointment reminders.
- Users want to add/edit/remove the medication refill reminders.
- Users want to add/edit/remove a doctor to their user profile.
- Users want to add/edit/remove a insurance information to their profile.
- Users want to add/edit/remove emergency contacts to their profile.
- Users want to create an account for their children who are under 17.
- Users want to see HIPPA regulations.

Persona's Created


Madeline Miller - Age: 13

Bio: Madeline is a 13-year-old female who has been diagnosed with asthma. Since Maddy is under 18 years old she will need her parents’ verification to create an account on MyNextLvl. She and her parents may create an account together. She gets irritated when she forgets she must miss school to go see the doctor, so her parents want to give her a way to get reminders. She also wants to learn more about asthma and why she needs an inhaler and learn about her body’s limitations when she plays outside.


Johnny Lawrence - Age: 19

Bio: Johnny is a 19-year-old young man who just graduated high school. He had been recently diagnosed with Hyperthyroidism. He wants a way to become more independent with his medical condition. He is looking for a way to organize his insurance information, doctor's appointments, and medications all together in one place. He would also like to know how this condition will affect the limitations on his body.


Deanna Colbert - Age: 36

Bio: Deanna has a 5-year-old son who has been diagnosed with diabetes. She wants to become more familiar with the condition so she can help manage his condition the best way possible. She also wants to be sure that her child's medical information is safe.
